About ACE

ACE (Association of Computer Engineers) is a student organization of the Computer Science and Engineering Department of S.R.K.R Engineering College, Bhimavaram. It was started in the year 2006 and since then it has been aiming towards enlightening the students in technical aspects as well as communication and soft-skills that are required in today's world.

Our Mission

At ACE, our mission is to provide a conducive platform for students to explore their potential and excel in both academic and extracurricular pursuits. We are committed to:

  • Technical Brilliance: Organizing workshops, seminars, and technical events to keep students updated with cutting-edge technologies and industry trends.
  • Cultural Enrichment: Nurturing artistic talents and celebrating cultural diversity through events like "Spurthi," our annual cultural fest.
  • Industry Connect: Bridging the gap between academia and industry by organizing guest lectures, industrial visits, and internships.
  • Innovation and Problem-Solving: Hosting hackathons and competitions, including the renowned "Prajwalan," where students collaborate to develop innovative solutions to real-world challenges.
  • Soft Skills Development: Recognizing the significance of communication and interpersonal skills, ACE conducts sessions and workshops to enhance the soft skills of its members.
  • Social Responsibility: Instilling a sense of social responsibility among students, ACE engages in community service activities.
